    • 《sonar flare ii》

    The night before my second performance, I scrapped the set that I had practiced for weeks and started over. The choir recording I had been using was challenging to make musical at every pitch. Instead, I wanted to try and move away from using music samples altogether and see what I could create with a similar setup as my first with no music.

    I took a recording of the highway overpass next to the Memphis Bass Pro Shop Pyramid in June 2022 mixed with a recording of a dawn chorus in Chatsworth Park in July 2022. processed live with a morphagene (make noise), stages (mutable instruments), a cold mac (mannequins), and a 2hp comb filter. Credit to Tuan Huang for the photos.
