    • 《sonar flare i》

    The concept for my first live performance centered around a guqin tune I was learning at the time -- 招隱, which can translate both to "Seeking Seclusion" and "Summoning the Recluse" (more info here). My plan was to record a take of myself performing the song to use as a sample, but my city apartment isn't exactly soundproof. One particularly loud practice session was recorded on a Saturday night in May 2022, with sirens criss-crossing the street outside and the subway rumbling every minute below. Since I wanted to continue perfecting the tune and the modular set in parallel, I went ahead and loaded it into the Morphagene. I soon realized that the sirens, the subway, and my own voice mumbling about the noise lent both texture and character to the piece, and I decided to use that practice session for the performance.

    For my performance, the sample was processed live with the morphagene (make noise) and the cold mac (mannequins) and sequenced by the stages (mutable instruments).
