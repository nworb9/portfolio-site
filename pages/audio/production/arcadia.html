    • arcadia inc.

    Arcadia Inc. (2021) is a project by Cezar Mocan involving a fictional company offering "context-free landscape photography for the 21st century user". I produced an original score to mix with the narrated essay, followed by a spin-off 3-track EP.

    For the score, we selected the guqin tune "Sigh for Antiquity", which focuses on the Chinese literati's romanticization of times past. I recorded a take of myself performing the song, granulated it with the Make Noise Morphagene, sequenced it with the Mutable Instruments Stages, and mixed it with field recordings from freesound.org. The result is a combination of synthesized traditional music alongside breaths of wind and cicadas.

    In 2021, Lizzy Chiappini curated a solo show featuring the work called softpower.exe. It was a part of the SPRING / BREAK Art Show in New York, as well as the CURRENTS New Media Art Show in Santa Fe.
