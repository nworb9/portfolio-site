    • 《arcadia inc.》

    Arcadia Inc. (2021) is a project by Cezar Mocan focused on a fictional company offering "context-free landscape photography for the 21st century user". I produced an original 10-minute long score to mix with the narrated essay, which I released as a 3-track EP.

    For the score, we selected the guqin tune 慨古吟, or "Sigh for Antiquity", which focuses on the Chinese literati's romanticization of times past. I recorded a take of myself performing the song, granulated it with the Make Noise Morphagene, sequenced it with the Mutable Instruments Stages, and mixed it with field recordings from freesound.org. The result is synthetic traditional music and whispers of wind and cicadas.

    In 2021, Lizzy Chiappini curated a solo show featuring the work called softpower.exe. It was a part of the SPRING / BREAK Art Show in New York, as well as the CURRENTS New Media Art Show in Santa Fe.
