    • ambiences

    I began recording things during the pandemic after I started micing up my guqin for online lessons with a borrowed microphone. I didn't know anything about best practices while recording acoustic instruments or anything about EQing and post-production. The first thing I noticed was how much more accurately I could hear my slide notes. The second thing I noticed was how loud and unique the sounds of my shared apartment were.

    I learned to embrace the honesty and imperfections of my early recordings, including my amateur guqin practice, the sounds of my flatmates in the apartment, as well as the sounds of the neighborhood outside. Eventually, I began to capture more recordings outside to capture the unique ambiences of the surrounding areas.
