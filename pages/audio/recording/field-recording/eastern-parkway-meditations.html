    • eastern parkway meditations

    eastern parkway meditations (2021 - present) is an ongoing field recording project involving recordings taken in my apartment or along Eastern Parkway in Brooklyn. It began after moving into my apartment and noticing the faint rumble of the subway turning a corner beneath my building. Each of the tracks feature the unique white noise inherent to my block, including the building vent on my balcony and the parkway outside.
