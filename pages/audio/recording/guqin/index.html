    • 《guqin》

    I’ve been studying guqin since 2019 with Weeteng Su’s Cool Fragrance Qin Loft, based out of Singapore. She is of the Guanling Pai lineage. Since the pandemic, we have done early morning video lessons.

    I first discovered the instrument after attending an introductory class I was invited to by a friend, and I immediately fell in love with the quiet, bassy tone of the instrument.

    There are many challenges that come with learning only through video, and most of the resources for guqin are not yet translated to English. Progress is slow, but I still try to record regularly to capture improvement.
