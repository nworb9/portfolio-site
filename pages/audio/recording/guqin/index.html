    I’ve been studying guqin since 2019 with Weeteng Su’s Cool Fragrance Qin Loft, based out of Singapore. She is of the Guanling Pai lineage. Since the pandemic, we have done early morning video lessons. I first discovered the instrument after attending an introductory class I was invited to by a friend, and I immediately fell in love with the quiet, bassy tone of the instrument.
